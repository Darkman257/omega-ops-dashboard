# NEXUS Egyptian Compliance Guardrails: Scope
**System:** Omega Operations Dashboard

## ⚖️ Legal Disclaimer
- This system is **NOT** a legal advice engine or an automated compliance officer.
- All logic provided is for **operational guardrail** purposes only.
- Any salary calculations, tax deductions, or insurance enrollment data MUST be reviewed by a certified Egyptian accountant or legal counsel before execution.

## 🛡️ Core Principles
1. **Data Integrity:** No hard deletes for Staff, Fleet, or Housing. Use status markers.
2. **Manual Entry Guard:** All manual staff additions require a "Compliance Checklist" verification.
3. **Financial Isolation:** Payroll, Social Insurance, and Tax fields are `Requires Approval` or `Blocked` for direct edits.
4. **Offboarding Compliance:** No staff deletion without a "Clearance Form" (إخلاء طرف) check.
